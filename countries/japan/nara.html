Gluten-Free Survival Guide

Key Phrases (Japanese)
私はセリアック病です。小麦、大麦、ライ麦を食べられません。 I have celiac disease
グルテンフリーのメニューはありますか？ Do you have a GF menu?
醤油は小麦が入っていますか？ Does soy sauce contain wheat?
Grocery Stores
  • Supermarket under JR Nara Station — GF pasta, fresh sushi, salads
  • Seijo Ishii — imported GF products
  • Bio-Ral — organic/GF options
Safe Cuisines
  • Plain rice, onigiri (check fillings)
  • Sashimi (use GF tamari, not regular soy)
  • Yakitori with salt (shio) — skip tare
  • Mochi/dango (rice-based, but check for wheat flour)
  • Sake (naturally GF — Nara is birthplace of sake!)

Getting Around

Kintetsu Nara Line
Fastest from Kyoto (35 min, ¥1,280) or Osaka-Namba (36 min, ¥680). Station steps from Kofuku-ji and Nara Park.
Best for most visitors
JR Yamatoji Line
From Kyoto (45 min, ¥720) or Osaka (45 min, ¥840). Covered by Japan Rail Pass. JR Nara Station 15–20 min walk from park.
Best with JR Pass
Nara Loop Bus
Runs frequently from both stations to main sights. Convenient if you want to save energy for temples.
¥100–220/ride
Walking
Main sights clustered around Nara Park. Temple loop from Kintetsu Station is 5–7 km. Flat and pleasant.
The best way
Local Tip
Take Kintetsu over JR unless you have a JR Pass — Kintetsu Nara Station is right next to the sights, while JR Nara is a 15–20 minute walk away.

Must-See Spots

4 spots

Todai-ji Temple

Historic / Temple
Home to Great Buddha (14.98m bronze, 752 CE). Main hall is largest wooden structure in world. Don’t miss Nigatsu-do for city views. UNESCO.
East Nara Park
Traveler recommended

Nara Park

Nature / Park
1,200+ wild deer, considered sacred. Buy shika senbei (¥200). Deer bow for treats! Some are aggressive and may bite.
Central Nara

Kofuku-ji

Historic / Temple
Ancient temple (669 CE), famous five-story pagoda. Beautifully lit at night. Steps from Kintetsu Nara Station.

Kasuga Taisha Shrine

Historic / Shrine
Shinto shrine (768 CE), thousands of stone/bronze lanterns. All lit during Mantoro festivals (Feb & Aug). Surrounded by 1,000-year-old Kasugayama Primeval Forest (UNESCO).

Travel Tips

  • Nara is best as a day trip from Kyoto (35 min) or Osaka (36 min) — 4–6 hours for highlights
  • Take Kintetsu over JR unless you have a JR Pass — Kintetsu station is much closer to sights
  • Visit Todai-ji first thing to beat tour groups
  • Buy deer crackers from official vendors only (¥200) — deer know who has them!
  • Walk through Naramachi after temples for quieter charming neighborhood
  • Sarasojyu and Naramachi Vegan Nabi are both in Naramachi — plan lunch there
  • Sake is naturally GF — Nara birthplace of sake, rare treat for celiac travelers
  • Most restaurants outside dedicated spots won’t understand celiac — bring dining card and GF soy sauce
